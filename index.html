02 / Fee Structure

What is different about our fee structure?

Most personal injury law firms charge one fee if your case settles and a higher fee if a lawsuit must be filed. At the Law Office of Salah Harrell, we believe injured people should not be penalized because the insurance company refuses to be reasonable. That is why our fee remains 25%, whether your case settles before a lawsuit or requires litigation. The percentage never increases simply because more work becomes necessary. Our clients know exactly what they will pay from day one.

We created our fee structure because we understand the financial pressure many South Georgia families face. Groceries cost more. Gas costs more. Housing costs more. When an injury disrupts your life, the last thing you need is to lose an even larger portion of your recovery. By limiting our fee to 25%, more of the settlement remains where we believe it belongs — in your pocket.

05 / Quality

What is the quality of representation?

A lower fee does not mean lower-quality representation. Every case receives the same preparation, investigation, and advocacy regardless of the percentage charged. We gather evidence, obtain medical records, communicate with insurance companies, and build claims designed to maximize recovery. Our responsibility is to pursue the best possible outcome for every client. The amount we charge does not change our commitment to excellence.

Insurance companies are sophisticated businesses with experienced adjusters and defense lawyers. Successfully handling injury claims requires knowledge, preparation, and persistence. We approach each case with the understanding that every dollar recovered can make a meaningful difference in a client’s life. Whether negotiating a settlement or presenting a case in court, we advocate aggressively for our clients’ interests. We prepare every case with the expectation that it may require litigation.

Our clients receive representation grounded in professionalism, communication, and dedication. We believe people should be treated with dignity and respect throughout the legal process. That means returning calls, providing updates, and making sure clients understand what is happening with their case. We are committed to earning trust through our actions, not just our promises. Our goal is to deliver exceptional legal representation while helping clients keep more of what they recover.
